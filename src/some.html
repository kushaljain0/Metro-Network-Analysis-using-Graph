Latitude and longitude of Central Secretariat metro station

  • In India

 Satellite map of Central Secretariat metro station

The Central Secretariat (Hindi: केन्द्रीय सचिवालय ) (often abbreviated Central Sectt on platforms and trains) is a Delhi Metro station in Delhi, on the Yellow Line. The Violet Line links it with Badarpur. The station provides a same-level interchange between the two lines.

Latitude: 28° 36' 54.15" N
Longitude: 77° 12' 42.43" E

Nearest city to this article: New Delhi
